OUTSTANDING CARE IS WHAT SETS US APART

Comprehensive Care for the Whole Family

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry can encompass things such as tooth-colored fillings, dental implants, or crowns. We have many options, so please call us for a free cosmetic consultation.

A beautiful, bright, healthy smile can increase self confidence and overall life satisfaction.

CFast® Braces

A fast and cosmetic treatment designed to straighten your teeth in as little as four to six months. Cfast® braces use nickel-titanium wires to exert pressure on clear brackets bonded to your teeth to level and align them. Call us today for a free consultation!

Lumineers® Prepless Veneers

Veneers are very thin porcelain pieces that are custom made to adhere directly to the front of teeth without grinding or shaving. Transform your smile without drilling on your natural teeth!

In the hands of a highly skilled dentist, Lumineers® can provide you with an incredibly natural, beautiful smile. They are virtually undetectable and can dramatically improve your appearance. They are long-lasting and provide a quicker, pain-free, reversible approach to veneers.

Inlays and Onlays

Dr. LeCavalier strives to conserve your own healthy tooth structure whenever possible. For those who have worn or cracked teeth, or who have large silver fillings, Dr. xxx recommends porcelain inlays. The porcelain bonds directly to the tooth enamel which means it is both durable and beautiful. An onlay is used when the tooth structure may be compromised by the use of a crown. Ask us about a free consultation if you feel you may need an onlay or an inlay.

CEREC® One-Visit Crowns

CEREC® technology allows our practice to create tooth-colored porcelain restorations for healthy, beautiful smiles in a single appointment..

Crowns should be placed before the tooth is so decayed that it may fracture. This can often help prevent the expense of root canal therapy in the future. If a tooth is too damaged to have a crown applied, a porcelain bridge may be a good alternative.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation dentistry is an excellent treatment for patients with extensive dental needs, or who are concerned about anxiety, difficulty getting numb, or sensitive teeth.

Smile-Whitening

Many everyday things like coffee, tea and wine can significantly dull teeth. Smile-whitening enables us to significantly brighten your smile for that dazzling white smile you deserve.

Plastic whitening trays will be made from models of your teeth. You will then place a special whitening gel in each tray and wear the trays in your mouth for several hours per day. (Many patients wear their trays after dinner and before bed.) A significant change in tooth color is usually seen in just four to six weeks.
