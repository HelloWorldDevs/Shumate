Meet Your Dental Team

The minute you walk through the door, you will know you've come to the right place. The office's comforting atmosphere and inviting team members will make you feel right at home, while some of the latest advances in dentistry, including digital x-rays and tooth-colored restorations help you maintain a beautiful, healthy smile.

Dana B. Shumate, DDS, PA, General Dentist

Dr. Dana Shumate would like to welcome you to a fantastic dental experience! By combining advanced dental technology with a gentle, friendly style, Dr. Shumate and his team will help make dentistry fun and exciting for your family. Dr. Shumate takes the time to get to know each and every patient one-on-one, helping you make informed choices about your dental health.

A graduate of West Virginia University School of Dentistry, Dr. Shumate attends extensive postdoctoral studies in clinical dentistry, focusing on cosmetics and family dentistry. He has presented research across the country receiving recognition from both the Hinman Dental Society and the American Academy for Oral and Maxillofacial Radiology. He currently is a member of the American Dental Association, North Carolina Dental Society, Tri-County Dental Society, International Dental Implant Association, and the Dental Organization for Conscious Sedation. Dr. Shumate and his wife, Jennifer, have been married twelve years and have three children.

Charlotte Hagan, Certified Dental Assistant

Charlotte is a native of Wilmington, NC. She graduated from Cape Fear Community College with a degree in Dental Assisting. Charlotte is married and has two children of her own, and two stepchildren. In her free time, she enjoys spending time with her family, boating, fishing, and loves traveling.

Rhonda Kelly, Registered Dental Hygienist

Rhonda graduated in 2008 from Midlands Technical College with an associates in dental hygiene. She finished her bachelor degree in health science in 2010. Rhonda is happily married with one daughter. In her free time she enjoys playing games, time with family and friends, and traveling.

Jennifer Shumate, Office Manager

Jennifer grew up in Texas and West Virginia where she attended high school and met Dr. Shumate. She graduated from West Virginia University with a Bachelors degree in Child Development and Family Studies, and went on to get her Master's degree in Elementary Education. Jennifer is the office administrator and works hard to ensure your experience is a memorable one. Jennifer enjoys kayaking, paddle boarding, going to the beach, spending time with her family, and reading when she gets the chance.
